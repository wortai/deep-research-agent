# Technical & Engineering Manual Layout

**Vibe**: Brutalist, schematic, blueprint style, zero wasted space, strict Technical Minimalist.
**Colors**: 
- Background: 'Paper' (`#F7F7F5`).
- Text: 'Grid' (`#3A3A38`).
- Accents & Hazard: 'Coral' (`#FF8C69`) for hard warnings. 'Forest Green' (`#1A3C2B`) for standard borders.
- No gradients, shadows, or rounded corners anywhere. 

**Typography**:
- Font-Family: Exclusively 'JetBrains Mono' for maximum schematic density and alignment, or paired strictly with 'Space Grotesk' for heavy titling.
- Text is dense, precise line-height. Numeric hierarchies are dominant.

**Components**:
- Bounded Elements: Everything mapped into a strict grid layout bound by 1px hairlines (`#3A3A38` at 20% opacity).
- `table`: Blueprint style. Hard 1px lines for every cell. No zebra striping, just pure grids. JetBrains Mono across all cells.
- `blockquote`: "CAUTION" boxes. 1px solid Coral border, flat Coral tint if absolutely necessary, JetBrains Mono tag overhead.
- Headings: Uppercase, heavily letter-spaced, boxed in or underlined. Absolute numbering (`1.1`, `1.1.2`, etc.) written in JetBrains Mono.
- Diagrams: 1px border. `mix-blend-luminosity` (black & white rendering) style.

**Focus**: Pure data transmission, safety warnings, exact specification tables, high-density schematic design.
