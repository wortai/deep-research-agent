# Technical & Engineering Manual Layout

**Vibe**: Brutalist, schematic, utilitarian, dense but hyper-organized, blueprint style.
**Colors**: 
- Background: Draft Paper Gray (`#EEEEEE`) or Pure White (`#FFFFFF`)
- Text: Pure Black (`#000000`)
- Borders/Lines: Blueprint Blue (`#0033A0`) or Hazard Orange (`#FF5722`) for warnings.
- No gradients or shadows. Completely flat.

**Typography**:
- Font-Family: Monospace or strict geometric sans-serif ('Roboto Mono', 'Helvetica Neue').
- Text is dense, line-height `1.4`. No fluff.

**Components**:
- All elements bound by visible boxes.
- `table`: Spreadsheet style. Hard black lines for every cell. No zebra striping, just pure grids.
- `blockquote`: "CAUTION" or "WARNING" boxes. Thick border (`4px solid red` or orange), bold, uppercase text inside.
- Headings: Uppercase, heavily letter-spaced, boxed in or underlined with a harsh `2px` border. Numbering is absolute (`1.1`, `1.1.2`, etc.).
- Images/Diagrams: Black and white borders.

**Focus**: Pure data transmission, safety warnings, exact specification tables, zero wasted space.
