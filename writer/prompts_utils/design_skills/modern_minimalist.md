# Minimalist & Modern Layout

**Vibe**: Technical Minimalist, essentialist, heavily whitespace-driven, "less is more" but highly engineered.
**Colors**: 
- Background: 'Paper' (`#F7F7F5`).
- Text: 'Grid' (`#3A3A38`).
- Headings: 'Forest' (`#1A3C2B`).
- Accents: Minimal use of 'Gold' (`#F4D35E`) or 'Mint' (`#9EFFBF`) for precise highlights.

**Typography**:
- Font-Family: 'Space Grotesk' for stark, structural headers. 'General Sans' for highly legible body copy.
- Structure/Metadata: 'JetBrains Mono' (small sizes, tight styling) for technical indicators and dates.
- Perfect, crisp scaling. Enormous margins separating blocks of text.

**Components**:
- Everything is spaced generously (`3rem` or `4rem`). Breathing room is the primary design tool.
- Borders: Exclusively 1px hairlines (`#3A3A38` at 20% opacity) for structural divisions. No box-shadows.
- `blockquote`: 1px solid border or no border. JetBrains Mono label above the quote.
- `h1`: Tiny overhead labels in JetBrains Mono (`font-size: 10px; text-transform: uppercase; letter-spacing: 0.1em`) preceding a short title in Space Grotesk.
- Tables: Invisible borders except for a single thin 1px line under the header. 0px border-radius everywhere.

**Focus**: Reading comfort, brutal but premium simplicity, geometric layout, letting the content speak naturally.
