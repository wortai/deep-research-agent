# Minimalist & Modern Layout

**Vibe**: Scandinavian, heavily whitespace-driven, stark, essentialist, "less is more".
**Colors**: 
- Background: White (`#FFFFFF`) or Warm Light Gray (`#F9F9F9`)
- Text: Subdued Charcoal (`#4A4A4A`)
- Headings: Sharp Black (`#000000`)
- Little to no accent colors. Contrast relies on grayscale.

**Typography**:
- Font-Family: Pure neo-grotesques ('Helvetica Now', 'Inter', 'SF Pro Display').
- Perfect, crisp scaling. Enormous margins separating blocks of text.

**Components**:
- Everything is spaced by `3rem` or `4rem`. Breathing room is the design.
- No borders, no shadows. Separation is achieved entirely through layout and padding.
- `blockquote`: No borders. Just significantly larger font size, italicized, pushed heavily to the right or center.
- `h1`: Tiny overhead labels (e.g., `font-size: 10px; text-transform: uppercase; letter-spacing: 2px`) preceding a massive, short title.
- Tables: Invisible borders except for a single thin black line under the header. 

**Focus**: Reading comfort, brutal simplicity, letting the content speak completely for itself.
