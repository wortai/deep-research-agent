The style is 'Technical Minimalist'. It pairs a sans-serif header (Space Grotesk) with a monospaced functional font (JetBrains Mono) for technical metadata. The color scheme revolves around 'Paper' (#F7F7F5), 'Forest' (#1A3C2B), and 'Grid' (#3A3A38). It avoids all depth effects (shadows, gradients) in favor of 1px border-lines and flat color blocks. Micro-interactions are snappy with linear or standard ease-out curves.

Spec
Background: 'Paper' (#F7F7F5) with a seamless mosaic SVG overlay.

Primary: 'Forest Green' ( #1A3C2B).

Typography: 'Space Grotesk' for bold, tight-tracking headers; 'General Sans' for chat prose/body; 'JetBrains Mono' for labels, tags, and small technical metadata (font-size: 10px-12px, tracking: 0.1em).

Borders: 1px hairlines in #3A3A38 at 20% opacity for all dividers and containers.

Palette: Primary Forest (#1A3C2B), Coral (#FF8C69), Mint (#9EFFBF), and Gold (#F4D35E).

Styling: No box shadows. All components have 0px or 2px (sm) border-radius.

Images: Use mix-blend-luminosity with a 90% opacity, shifting to full color on hover (used specifically for Founder Portraits).
