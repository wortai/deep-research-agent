## Overview 🌟
The GitHub Research Agent is a powerful research tool that leverages specialized AI models to conduct in-depth analysis and provide actionable insights. With three distinct search modes—**WebSearch**, **DeepSearch**, and **ExtremeSearch**—it adapts to the complexity of your query, ensuring both speed and depth. Whether you're a researcher, developer, or business professional, this agent is your go-to solution for data-driven decision-making. 🧠

## Key Features ✨
- **Multi-Domain Expertise**: Conducts research in finance, coding, biology, business, market analysis, and custom topics.
- **Three Search Modes**: Choose from WebSearch, DeepSearch, or ExtremeSearch based on your research needs.
- **Specialized Models**: Utilizes tailored AI models optimized for specific tasks to ensure high-quality results.
- **Customizable**: Easily configure the agent to focus on niche or user-defined topics.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Simple setup and intuitive commands for seamless interaction.

## Search Modes 🕵️‍♂️
The Research Agent offers three distinct modes to cater to varying levels of research depth:

### WebSearch 🌐
- Ideal for quick, surface-level insights.
- Pulls real-time data from the web for fast results.
- Perfect for general queries or preliminary research.

### DeepSearch 🔬
- Conducts thorough analysis with iterative data collection.
- Combines multiple sources for comprehensive insights.
- Suited for complex topics requiring detailed exploration.

### ExtremeSearch ⚡
- Maximum depth for highly specialized or niche queries.
- Leverages advanced models for exhaustive research.
- Best for groundbreaking discoveries or mission-critical analysis.

## Supported Domains 🧬💻💰
The Research Agent excels in the following areas:
- **Finance** 💵: Market trends, investment strategies, and economic analysis.
- **Coding** 💻: Code debugging, algorithm optimization, and programming best practices.
- **Biology** 🧬: Genetic research, bioinformatics, and medical studies.
- **Business** 📈: Strategic planning, competitive analysis, and operational insights.
- **Market Analysis** 📊: Consumer trends, market forecasting, and industry reports.
- **Custom Topics** 🎯: Tailored research for user-defined or niche subjects.
